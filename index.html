About

Hello, my name is Andres Garcia. I am passionate about self-improvement. Coming from a first-generation Mexican-American family, I learned the values of hard work, discipline, and perseverance from my parents. In high school, I took a job at an auto repair shop with no prior knowledge about cars. Despite this, I quickly adapted and thrived in the role. This experience taught me that even when I have no idea where to start, I can learn, adapt, and succeed. It also inspired me to embrace new challenges with confidence and determination.

After high school, I went to college to pursue a computer science degree, but the cost was very high, and it would take a long time. Given my circumstances, I needed a more practical, hands-on way of learning to get into what I wanted to do. I decided to enroll in a software engineering bootcamp, App Academy. There, I learned all about the full development life cycle and implemented every step with full-stack applications. I enjoy building responsive UX/UI, implementing RESTful APIs, and taking on challenging projects. For example, I added WebSockets to one of my projects to enable real-time messaging, a feature I had to learn and implement on my own. I successfully completed the entire project and implemented WebSockets within two weeks, showcasing my ability to quickly learn and apply new technologies.

Some of my hobbies include weightlifting, playing video games, and spending quality time with friends and family. I am dedicated to self-improvement and constantly seeking opportunities to expand my knowledge and abilities. I am determined to learn, grow, and create a better future for myself and my family.

Pinfluence

GitHub Live Site

Pinfluence is a full-stack web app where users can create and manage boards, pins, and profiles. It supports real-time messaging using Flask-SocketIO and socket.io-client and offers a responsive interface built with React and Redux. The app is deployed via Docker and AWS S3 for media storage.

                     

Welp

GitHub Live Site

Welp is a full-stack, Yelp-inspired web application where users can browse or create businesses. It offers features such as advanced search and filter options to help users find and rate businesses easily. The platform ensures a user-friendly experience for discovering businesses and sharing reviews.
